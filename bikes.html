Vitus Sentier 27 Bike (Deore 1x10)

This brilliant hardtail is perfect for lawless mountain adventures as it offers phenomenal power and control on every ride. Ready for action, this model is built around a solid 6061 alloy frame construction which makes it ideal at tackling all the challenges that come with fast-paced trail riding. Vitus has also given this model boost hub spacing, so you can benefit from bigger and burlier tyres as you blast across the mountain.

X-Fusion RC32 Suspension Forks

Engineered for maximum off-road performance, this powerful workhorse is designed for both all-day epics and riding after work with friends. Keeping you riding with confidence, this version is kitted out with X-Fusion RC32 forks which absorb every bump and vibration as you shred. Furthermore, its Shimano Deore 10 speed gearing allows you to accelerate up to speed in a flash while its Tektro HD-M29 hydraulic disc brakes keep you in control as you weave from trail to trail.

Vitus Nucleus 27 VRS Bike (Deore 1x10)

The Nucleus VRS is a step above its sibling the Nucleus VR and features a 1x10-Speed drivetrain that's complete with a 11-46T wide range cassette to get you up the steepest hills effortlessly. It features a quality construction, solid spec list and an increased stand over height with aggressive geometry for immense control on the fastest descents while ensuring you easily get back to the top for another run back down. Vitus’ number one, best-selling hardtail, the Nucleus has won MBR’s under £500 hardtail of the year for the last four years running and it's easy to see why.

SR Suntour Suspension and Shimano 1x10 Drivetrain with 11-46T Cassette

With its low, long and slack geometry, the new Nucleus 27 VR's ride versatility is increased via 120mm of Suntour fork travel, Tektro hydraulic disc brakes and large volume WTB tyres on wide rims for outstanding rolling performance and grip. Plus, it's equipped with a reliable and precise shifting 10-speed drivetrain from Shimano, a Vitus finishing kit and dropper post routing for easy installation of a dropper post upgrade in the future. This is a hardtail that’s built to take your riding to the next level and does so without breaking the bank.
